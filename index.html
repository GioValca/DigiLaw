Our Story

Hello, we are DigiLaw, and we are here to help small to medium sized businesses to comply with the ever changing laws of our digitalised society. Rules as Code means translating the complex human language of privacy regulations into logical code that can easily be checked by machines. Using such code our software can automatically check any system for GDPR compliance and tell what you would need to change and how.
